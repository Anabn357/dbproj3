Critic Score Residual

  • From the above analysis, it is seen that all the models gives almost similar result with Critic Score. Yet the Best Training Model was found to be the "Ridge Model" as the MSE is lower than other models and the R2 score is higher by few points. With the MSE : 0.77 and R2: 0.14, we can say that the Error Value is quite high and the Ridge Score is too low therefore we can conclude that the Critic Scores are not a very reliable predictor of the future success of the video game sales.
